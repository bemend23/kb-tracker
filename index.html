Technique Checklist
Quick scan before sets. Keep reps crisp. Stop when form degrades.
KB Swing (hinge)
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: “Hike” the bell back, snap hips, ribs down, neutral spine, arms as hooks.
  • Feel: glutes/hamstrings + abs; NOT low back.
  • Common mistakes: squatting it, lifting with arms, overextending at top, bell drifting far away.
  • Self-check: top position = tall plank (glutes tight, abs tight), not a backbend.
KB Clean & Press
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: keep bell close, “zipper path,” punch through to rack (no wrist slam), brace before press.
  • Rack: forearm vertical-ish, wrist neutral, elbow near ribs, lat engaged.
  • Common mistakes: casting bell away, floppy wrist, pressing without bracing, shrugging to finish.
  • Scaling: if strict press breaks → push press for last minutes.
Goblet Squat
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: feet rooted, chest tall, elbows inside knees, sit between heels.
  • Common mistakes: knees collapsing in, heels lifting, rounding at the bottom.
  • Fix: slow 2–3 sec down; pause 1 sec in the hole if needed.
Push-up
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: body as one unit, glutes tight, ribs down, hands under shoulders, elbows ~30–45°.
  • Common mistakes: sagging hips, flaring elbows, craning neck.
  • Scaling: elevate hands (counter/bench) to keep quality reps.
Single-leg KB RDL
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: hips square, soft knee, reach back with free leg, feel hamstring stretch, slow down.
  • Common mistakes: opening pelvis, rounding back, collapsing arch/ankle.
  • Fix: touch a wall with rear foot lightly for balance while keeping hinge pattern.
Bodyweight Row (table / rail / TRX)
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: rigid plank body, pull elbows toward hips, pause at top, control down.
  • Common mistakes: shrugging, hips sagging, yanking reps.
  • If unavailable: swap in prone Y-T-W raises (2×10 each) or towel isometric rows.
