Technique Checklist
Quick scan before sets. Keep reps crisp. Stop when form degrades.
KB Swing (hinge)
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: hike back, snap hips, ribs down, neutral spine, arms as hooks.
  • Feel: glutes/hamstrings + abs; NOT low back.
  • Mistakes: squatting it, lifting with arms, backbending at top, bell drifting away.
KB Clean & Press
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: keep bell close, “zipper path,” punch-through to rack, brace before press.
  • Mistakes: casting bell away, wrist slam, pressing without bracing.
  • Scaling: strict press → push press when fatigue rises.
Goblet Squat
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: feet rooted, chest tall, elbows inside knees, sit between heels.
  • Mistakes: knees cave, heels lift, butt-wink/rounding at bottom.
Push-up
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: body as one unit, glutes tight, ribs down, elbows ~30–45°.
  • Mistakes: sagging hips, flaring elbows, craning neck.
Single-leg KB RDL
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: hips square, soft knee, slow down, feel hamstring stretch.
  • Mistakes: opening pelvis, rounding back, collapsing arch/ankle.
Bodyweight Row (table / rail / TRX)
🎥 Video demo
  • Cues: rigid plank body, pull elbows toward hips, control down.
  • Mistakes: shrugging, hips sagging, yanking reps.
